## What Is CoolTech+ Technology?

CoolTech+ is Petronas' proprietary additive and base-oil formulation designed to reduce engine operating temperatures. According to Petronas Lubricants International (PLI), CoolTech+ works by:

1. **Reduced internal friction** — Lower friction between moving parts generates less heat
2. **Superior heat dissipation** — The oil absorbs and transfers heat away from hotspots (bearings, turbo, piston rings) more efficiently
3. **Thermal stability** — The molecular structure resists breakdown at high temperatures, maintaining viscosity protection longer
4. **Deposit control** — Prevents varnish and sludge buildup that insulate surfaces and trap heat

**Does CoolTech+ actually work?**
Independent lab tests and real-world monitoring show Syntium 7000 maintains viscosity within grade specifications at 10,000 km in Malaysian conditions — which is the practical test that matters. Whether the temperature reduction claim is 3°C or 5°C as Petronas markets is harder to verify independently, but the oil demonstrably maintains its grade and delivers strong wear protection across its full service interval.

## Performance in Malaysian Conditions

### Climate suitability: Excellent
The 0W-20 viscosity is ideal for Malaysia:
- **"0W" winter rating**: Ultra-low cold viscosity for instant lubrication at startup. Even though Malaysia doesn't have cold winters, the benefit is faster oil circulation during morning cold starts (engines are "cold" at 25–30°C relative to 90°C operating temperature)
- **"20" operating rating**: Low operating viscosity for reduced friction and improved fuel efficiency. This is the viscosity most modern Japanese and Malaysian-assembled engines are designed for

### Fuel economy impact
Switching from 5W-30 or 5W-40 to 0W-20 (where manufacturer-approved) typically improves fuel economy by **2–5%** in real-world driving. On a car averaging 12 km/L, that's approximately:
- **300–600 ml less fuel per 100 km**
- **RM 100–250 savings per year** at current fuel prices (RON 95 at RM 2.05/L)
- The oil upgrade essentially pays for itself through fuel savings

### Durability at 10,000 km intervals
In our assessment of Malaysian driving conditions (urban traffic, 30–35°C ambient, 70%+ humidity), Syntium 7000 maintains:
- Viscosity within 0W-20 grade specification through 10,000 km
- Adequate TBN (total base number) to neutralize acids for the full interval
- Clean piston ring grooves and valve train — minimal varnish formation

**For severe conditions** (Grab/e-hailing, very short trips, extensive idling), consider shortening the interval to 7,000–8,000 km.

### How to verify authenticity
Counterfeit Petronas oil exists in the Malaysian market. Protect yourself:

1. **QR code scan** — Every genuine Petronas Syntium bottle has a QR code. Scan with the Petronas Lubricants app or camera.
2. **Security seal** — Tamper-proof cap seal should be intact
3. **Label quality** — Genuine labels are sharp, high-resolution print with consistent colour. Fakes often have blurry text or misaligned labels.
4. **Price check** — If it's under RM 100 for 4L of Syntium 7000, it's likely fake. Genuine retail doesn't go below RM 130 even during sales.
5. **Batch code** — Genuine bottles have a laser-engraved batch code on the bottle (not printed on a sticker)

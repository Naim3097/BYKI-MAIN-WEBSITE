## The Counterfeit Problem in Malaysia

Engine oil counterfeiting is a significant problem in Malaysia. Fake oil can cause:
- Accelerated engine wear (wrong viscosity, poor additive package)
- Voided warranty (if detected during warranty claim inspection)
- Engine failure in extreme cases (no anti-wear protection under load)

### Most counterfeited brands (highest to lowest risk):
1. **Mobil 1** — Global #1 counterfeit target
2. **Castrol EDGE** — Very high in Southeast Asia
3. **Shell Helix Ultra** — Moderate to high
4. **Petronas Syntium** — Moderate (growing with popularity)
5. **Idemitsu / Eneos / Liqui Moly** — Low (not well-known enough to counterfeit profitably)

### How to protect yourself:
1. **Buy from official sources** — Shopee Mall, LazMall, brand-owned stores, authorized workshops
2. **Verify QR codes** — Petronas, Shell, and Castrol all have authentication systems
3. **Check batch codes** — Should be laser-engraved, not printed on a sticker
4. **Inspect packaging** — Genuine products have consistent print quality, tamper seals, and correct spelling
5. **Price check** — If a "premium full synthetic" costs less than RM 80/4L, it's almost certainly fake

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: Does it matter what brand I use, or just the spec?
A: For most Malaysian driving, the specification (API SP, 0W-20) matters more than the brand. Any API SP certified oil from a reputable brand will protect your engine adequately. The differences between brands are marginal in normal driving — you'd need a laboratory to measure them. Choose based on price, availability, and counterfeit risk rather than marketing claims.

### Q: Can I switch brands at each oil change?
A: Yes — all API-certified oils are designed to be fully compatible regardless of brand. You can use Petronas one change, Shell the next, Idemitsu after that, with zero issues. The oils mix safely, and no "flushing" is needed between brands.

### Q: Why do some workshops insist on specific brands?
A: Workshops often have exclusive supply agreements with oil distributors. A Shell-affiliated workshop will push Shell products; a Petronas AutoExpert will push Petronas. This doesn't mean other brands won't work — it means the workshop has commercial incentives. Most bengkel workshops let you bring your own oil and just charge labour.

### Q: Is Malaysian Petronas oil the same quality as Petronas sold overseas?
A: Yes — Petronas Lubricants International manufactures to global standards. The Syntium 7000 sold in Malaysia meets the same API SP/ILSAC GF-6A specs as what's sold in Europe, the Middle East, and Southeast Asia. The R&D centre is in Italy, and formulations are consistent globally.

### Q: I have a motorcycle — can I use car engine oil?
A: **No.** Car engine oils contain friction modifiers that can cause motorcycle wet clutch slippage. Use oil specifically formulated for motorcycles (JASO MA/MA2 certified). Petronas Sprinta, Shell Advance, and Castrol Power1 are popular motorcycle oil lines in Malaysia.

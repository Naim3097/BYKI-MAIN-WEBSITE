# Module 11 — Petronas Syntium Products

## 11.1 The Petronas Syntium Range

**Petronas Syntium** is the passenger car engine oil range from Petronas Lubricants International (PLI). The range spans from entry-level mineral oils to the flagship 7000 series full synthetics — the same technology platform used in Formula 1 with the Mercedes-AMG Petronas F1 Team.

BYKI exclusively recommends Petronas Syntium engine oils and Petronas Tutela transmission fluids.

## 11.5 How to Choose the Right Product

### Step 1 — Check Your Owner's Manual

Your vehicle's owner's manual specifies the required:
- Viscosity grade (e.g., 0W-20)
- API or ILSAC minimum grade (e.g., API SP, ILSAC GF-6A)
- ACEA category (European vehicles)
- OEM-specific approval (e.g., MB 229.5, BMW LL-01)

### Step 2 — Match the Specification

Find the Petronas Syntium product that meets or exceeds all required specifications. The tables above list all specifications for each product.

### Step 3 — Or Let BYKI Choose

The easiest method: **run a BYKI health scan**. BYKI knows your vehicle's specifications and will recommend the correct Syntium product automatically, along with the right Tutela transmission fluid.

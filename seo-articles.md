- **Primary:** Malaysian vehicle owners (25–55 years old) searching for car problems, diagnostics, oil, and maintenance in English and Bahasa Melayu

### 1.3 Keyword Research Framework

**Where Malaysians search for car problems:**
- Google: "kereta [problem]", "car [symptom] causes", "[car model] [issue]"
- TikTok / YouTube: "kenapa kereta [symptom]"
- Forums: Paultan.org, Lowyat, Facebook groups
- AI search: ChatGPT, Google AI Overview, Perplexity

**High-value keyword patterns:**
- `[symptom] + causes` — "car jerking causes"
- `[car model] + [problem]` — "Myvi overheating problem"
- `[fault code]` — "P0171"
- `[fault code] + [car model]` — "P0420 Honda City"
- `best oil for [car model]` — "best oil for Perodua Axia"
- `[car model] + maintenance cost` — "Proton X50 maintenance cost"
- `how to [action]` — "how to check engine oil"
- `[service] + cost + Malaysia|Shah Alam|Selangor` — "car service cost Shah Alam"

## 6. Technical SEO Specifications

### 6.1 URL Structure

```
/blog/{slug}                    → All articles
/dtc/{code}                     → Auto-generated DTC pages
/vehicles/{make}/{model}        → Vehicle-specific pages
/oil-guide/{subtopic}           → Oil guide subsections
/learn/{module-slug}            → Knowledge base pages
/workshop/{location-or-slug}    → Workshop pages
```

- All lowercase, hyphenated
- No dates in URLs (evergreen content)
- No unnecessary nesting (max 3 levels)
- Trailing slashes: consistent (no trailing slash)

  "@context": "https://schema.org",

- [ ] **H2/H3 headings** as questions or clear topic labels

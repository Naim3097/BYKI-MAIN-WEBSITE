# Module 14 — Workshop & Booking

## 14.1 BYKI Partner Workshops

BYKI Partner Workshops are independent automotive service centres that have joined the BYKI network. These workshops:

- **Accept BYKI health reports** as a basis for service recommendations
- **Stock Petronas Syntium** products for oil changes and services
- **Use standardised pricing** for transparency
- **Report service completion** back to BYKI for your vehicle history

## 14.2 Finding a Workshop

After a Health Scan, or from the Home screen:

1. Tap **Book Workshop**
2. BYKI shows nearby Partner Workshops based on your location
3. Each workshop card displays:
   - Workshop name and address
   - Distance from your location
   - Operating hours
   - Services offered
   - Ratings and reviews
4. Tap a workshop to view full details and available time slots

## 14.3 Booking Flow

| Step | What Happens |
|------|-------------|
| **1. Select Workshop** | Choose a workshop from the map or list |
| **2. Select Service** | Pick the service type — oil change, diagnostic check, general service |
| **3. Choose Package** | Select a Syntium oil package (matched to your vehicle) |
| **4. Pick Date & Time** | Choose an available slot from the workshop's calendar |
| **5. Review & Confirm** | Review booking details, total cost estimate, and confirm |
| **6. Confirmation** | Receive booking confirmation with reference number |

## 14.5 Booking Confirmation

Your confirmed booking includes:

- **Reference number** for tracking
- **Workshop address** with map directions link
- **Date, time, and estimated duration**
- **Service package** selected and estimated cost
- **Reminder notifications** (24 hours and 1 hour before)

## 14.6 After Your Service

When the workshop completes your service:

1. Service details are recorded in your BYKI vehicle history
2. You receive a digital receipt
3. Your next service reminder is automatically set
4. You can rate and review the workshop

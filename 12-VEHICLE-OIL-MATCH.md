# Module 12 — Vehicle Oil Matching

## 12.1 How BYKI Matches Oil to Your Vehicle

BYKI's oil matching system considers:

1. **Vehicle make, model, and engine variant** — determines the manufacturer's specified oil
2. **OEM viscosity requirement** — the viscosity grade specified in the owner's manual
3. **API / ILSAC grade** — the minimum performance standard required
4. **ACEA category** — required for European vehicles
5. **OEM-specific approvals** — manufacturer certifications (MB 229.5, BMW LL-01, VW 502, etc.)
6. **Engine type** — naturally aspirated, turbocharged, diesel, hybrid
7. **Diagnostic data** — oil thermal stress, engine load patterns, and system health from the BYKI scan
